About

// Professional Profile

Dr. J S Saleema is an Associate Professor and Head of the Department of Statistics and Data Science at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bangalore. With 25 years of rich teaching experience and a PhD in Computer Science specializing in Data Mining and Machine Learning, she is passionate about developing sustainable healthcare analytics systems and fostering continuous learning in the academic community.

Her current research explores the neural mechanisms of brain behavior through cognitive neuroscience, investigating the impact of mindfulness on cognitive function and mental well-being.
